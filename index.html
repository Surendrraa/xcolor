Privacy Policy for Color Analyzer & Picker Toolkit

Effective Date: May 10, 2023

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Analyzer & Picker Toolkit ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our extension is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the extension to function properly:

  • Color History: The colors you pick and save are stored locally on your device using browser's local storage. This information never leaves your device.
  • Website Access: To enable the color picking functionality, our extension requires permission to access the current webpage you're viewing. This is necessary to identify and extract colors.
  • Extension Usage: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help improve our extension. This includes actions like how often the extension is opened or features are used. This data cannot be used to identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality of the extension, including color picking, analyzing, and saving color history.
  • Improve and optimize the extension's performance and user experience.
  • Develop new features based on how users interact with the extension.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties.

We may share aggregated, non-personal information for research and analysis purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data. All color data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access your saved colors through the extension interface at any time.
  • Delete: You can clear your color history through the extension settings.
  • Control: You can control which websites the extension has access to through your browser's extension settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Our extension may use Google Chrome's extension APIs to provide functionality. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles your data.

For the AI-powered features, we utilize algorithms that run locally within the extension. No image or website data is sent to external AI services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Extension Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab for color picking functionality.
  • storage: To save your color history and extension preferences locally.
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click functionality for accessing extension features.
